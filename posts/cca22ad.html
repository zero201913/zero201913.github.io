听力技巧

1.听前—读题(预测题目)选项不同之处是重点 过读音

2.听中-放音— 盯着题目 勾画你听到的内容-匹配程度高 为正确确选项(概率大)80%

3.听后-念题目—不听题目往下读选项

听力题1

A)They will see a 27% higher price to buy natural gas.
B)They will have insufficient heating oil to keep warm.
C)They will have a costly bill to pay to heat their homes. ×
D)They will experience the coldest winter in four decades.

A)Provided additional funding to Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program √
B)Assured U.S citizens of an increase in energy supplies by the end of this year.
C)Predicted a 10% jump in heating bills for most U.S households this winter
D)Decided on this year’s energy assistance package of$8 billion in total.

funding = money

assure = promise

jump 涨价

原文1

Across the U.S, families are looking to the winter with fear as energy costs soar. A number of factors are creating a bad situation, global energy consumption is up but the supplies have been reduced. the energy department projects heat bill will jump 28 percent this winter for those who rely on natural gas. Natural gas is used by nearly half of U.S households for heat. Heating oil is projected to be 27 percent higher. And electricity 10 percent higher. That comes against the inflation rates that accelerated last month with consumer prices growing 6.6 percent. that’s the fastest pace in 4 decades. The pain will be especially acute in part of the country that heavily rely on heating oil to keep homes warm. It is projected to cost more than 2300 dollars to heat a typical home with the heating oil this winter. last month, the federal government added one billion dollars in funding to low income energy assistance program. bringing the total to 4.8 billion dollars but that represents a reduction from last energy assistance package of over 8 billion dollars.

project 预估

acute 严重

听力操作

常见问题：

  • 没时间读题；
  • 熟词生义-题目听不懂；
  • 思考和听力不能同时进行
  • 不知道怎么记笔记

词汇量不够 - 熟练度

目标：把题做对，把文章听懂

听前：预读，猜测，过读音

听力题2

Questions 1 to 4 are based on the conversation you have just heard

A)Preparing for a job interview.
B)Writing a work report together.
C)Going through a couple of issues the company faces.
D)Discussing the woman’s annual performance review. ×

A)The overall culture of the company.
B)The honesty of the manager.
C)The instruction from her supervisor.
D)The recognition of her merits.

A)Her inadequate language proficiency.
B)Her inability to to interact with colleagues properly.
C)Her inappropriate behavior at company meetings
D)Her simplistic approach to dealing with others.

A)To avoid offending the recipients.
B)To show her unique writing style.
C)To be frank. D)To save time.

annual performance review 年度绩效评审

issue 问题

instruction 指示

merit 优点

inadequate 不充足的 - insufficient

proficiency 熟练程度，流利度 - fluency

inability 无能力的 - cannot

offend 冒犯

recipient 收件人

勾勾画画

（怎么就选了c，难搞ヽ(✿ﾟ▽ﾟ)ノ）

原文2

Ok then, this here is your yearly performance review. you’ve been with the company for three years. Under my direct supervision for the past 12 months. your report is mostly good. But there are a couple of issues we must to discuss. The report is yours to take with you. We’ll go through the main points together now, and you can come through it in greater detail later. Do you have any question before we start? No sir. I’ll get straight to it then. It is evident that you’re a hard worker. You are punctual and industrious. Furthermore, you have strong analytical skills and attention to detail. The company might self included a happy with your productivity and overall performance. Thank you, I appreciate the recognition. Do you agree then with this the depiction so far? Yeah, It’s a little simplistic perhaps. But that’s to be expected from this kind of evaluation. Sure, would you want to add anything? Your comments can be added to the report if you wish. Oh, there’s no need. It’s a flattering for portrayal really. Thank you, though. Ok, This leads me to the not so good part. This mostly centers around one behavior aspect you ability to work with others. I see. Yes, as you’ve already known, over the last several months. There have been some complained raised about your communication style. Of course, we’ve already have like 3 meetings about this. Look, I’m going to say is that it was never mind intention to offending anyone, simply put, I don’t like to write long email. Because frankly I think they’re a waste of time. conciseness is an efficiency. I feel that writing long emails hurts that same productivity you phrased me just a minute ago. and I already apologized if anyone was offended. But to be perfectly honest I think anyone who was offended was being a little to sensitive.

supervision监视 ，监督

come through经历困难后成功；传来；接通（电话）等意思

analytical分析的；善于分析的

straight to it直接着手做某事；直截了当地做

depiction描绘；描述；刻画

flattering奉承的；讨好的；使显得更漂亮的

portrayal描绘；描写；扮演；表现

center around以……为中心；围绕……

conciseness简洁；简明

phrased表达；措辞（phrase的过去式和过去分词形式）

听力题3

A. They experienced a heartbreaking moment.
B. They gathered together to rescue a elephant.
C. They heard a noise from the bottom of a well.
D. They found an elephant wandering around a well.

A. to borrow their crane
B. to ask them for help
C. to report the strange noise
D. to get food for the elephant

A. the elephant was able to return to the jungle
B. the army officers were examined for injuries.
C. the army officers were rewarded for their rescue efforts
D. the elephant was given medical treatment for many days.

heartbreaking 心痛的

well 井

crane 吊车，鹤

原文3

It was a heartwarming moment that dozens of Indians rescuers rally together to use a crane In order to save an elephant trapped at a bottom of a twenty feet well. The massive rescue efforts took place last week close to an army camp. The elephant wandered out of the jungle and fell into the well. The army officers heard a noise and coming from well and realized an elephant was trapped deep inside. The officers then throw in some food for the creature and call the local forest department for help. Video of the rescue operation shows how the team used a crane to life up the huge animal out of the well and onto back of the truck. It was then taken into an examining room for the evaluation of any injuries. The elephant was kept under close supervision for a few days. Before it was successfully released back into the wild.
